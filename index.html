Hello and welcome to my “How to luggage*” website.

If my experience as a frequent traveller has taught me anything, it’s that when it comes to luggage, there is a trade-off between price and quality, especially durability. And of course style! :-)

I used to buy suitcases which always broke after just a few uses - broken wheels and torn fabrics after a couple of flights or work trips. Yikes.

That’s the reason why I started to research and very carefully choose my bags and suitcases. I now get the most for my dollars (almost every time!) and travel in style, without worrying that the wheels might come off!

All of this led me to create this “how to luggage” website. I know it’s not grammatically correct, but it’s a pretty darn good description, isn’t it?
