What makes a great duffel bag

For travel, business or leisure, the single most important thing is that your duffel bag must fit all of your belongings. For most people we would recommend a bag with a size of at least 30 litres, which is more than enough to fit all of your gym stuff or a everything you need for a weekend getaway. But what makes a great duffel bag? To answer this question, we went through hundreds of products reviews and the analysis revealed that the following features separate a good suitcase from a great one:

  • Durable stitching is extremely important, particularly if you're planning to use the duffel bag on a daily basis.
  • If you're going to use the duffel to go to the gym or as a carry-on for a flight, make sure it will fit the gym locker or cabin.
  • Clever external and internal compartments can be very handy to organise all of your stuff and provide you with easy access to your keys, travel card, etc.
  • Shoulder straps with well made padding can make a real difference, especially if you carry heavy stuff.
  • Water resistant materials can come in quite handy for rainy days.

Things to watch out for

The analysis also revealed the things to watch out for when selecting your new duffel bag. Whilst none of these are deal-breakers, they are all things that can easily be avoided by carefully selecting your bag.

  • Not all duffels have adjustable straps or comfortable shoulder padding.
  • Avoid duffel bags which look stylish but have poor stitching as they are likely to last less than a month.
  • Watch out for the size of the bag as it varies significantly between different models and brands.
  • Hard-shell suitcases typically lack external pockets and have less packing space.
